3. How to Use Portify: Step-by-Step Guide
Step 1: Choose Your Template
Navigate to the "Generator" section. You'll see three template options displayed in the left panel:
Click the template dropdown to select your preferred design aesthetic
Consider your industry when selecting a template:
Executive: Best for finance, consulting, management roles
Startup: Best for creative, entrepreneurial, or modern tech roles
Technical: Best for engineering, data science, or technical roles
Step 2: Enter Your Personal Information
Fill in your basic professional details:
Full Name: Your professional name as it appears on LinkedIn
Headline: Your current role or desired position (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
Professional Summary: 2-3 sentences highlighting your background and value proposition
Email: Your professional contact email
LinkedIn URL: Link to your LinkedIn profile
Step 3: Upload a Profile Photo
Click the photo upload button and select a professional headshot. Recommended specifications:
Format: JPG or PNG
Size: 400x400 pixels or larger
Content: Professional headshot on neutral or solid background
The photo will be embedded directly in your HTML file. No external image hosting required.
Step 4: Add Your Professional Experience
Enter your work history. For each role, provide:
Role/Title: Your job title (e.g., "Product Manager")
Company: Organization name
Description: 1-2 sentences about your responsibilities and impact
Pro tip: Use action verbs and quantifiable results. Instead of "responsible for testing," write "reduced bug escape rate by 40% through automated testing framework implementation."
Step 5: Highlight Your Key Achievements
List 3-5 of your most impressive professional accomplishments. These should be:
Specific: Quantified when possible (percentages, numbers, scale)
Relevant: Aligned with the target role or industry
Impressive: Demonstrate impact and leadership
Example: "Led cross-functional team of 8 to launch new product feature, resulting in 25% increase in customer engagement."
Step 6: Add Your Core Skills/Strengths
List the technical and soft skills most relevant to your target role. Include:
Technical skills: Programming languages, tools, platforms
Domain expertise: Industry knowledge, certifications
Soft skills: Leadership, communication, project management
Step 7: Review the Live Preview
As you fill in information, the preview on the right updates in real-time. Use this to:
Verify all information displays correctly
Check that the template aesthetic matches your professional image
Ensure text length is appropriate and not cut off
Step 8: Download or Publish
You have three options:
Download HTML: Click "Download Site" to get a portfolio.html file
Copy Code: Click "Copy HTML" to copy the entire code to your clipboard
Publish with Netlify: Use Netlify Drop to instantly publish without setup

4. Best Practices for Maximum Impact
Content Strategy
Tailor your information to your target role. Before using Portify:
Review job descriptions for roles you're applying to. Extract keywords and emphasize relevant skills and achievements that match the job requirements.
Photo Guidelines
A professional photo increases engagement. Ensure:
Good lighting: Natural light or professional lighting setup
Professional attire: Business casual or formal dress appropriate to your industry
Clear background: Neutral or blurred background
Friendly expression: Approachable smile with direct camera contact
Writing Strong Achievements
Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result):
Situation: Context or challenge
Task: Your specific responsibility
Action: What you did to address it
Result: Measurable outcome or impact
Example: "(Situation) Team faced 30% churn rate. (Task) Led retention strategy overhaul. (Action) Implemented personalized onboarding and support. (Result) Reduced churn to 15% within 6 months."
Template Selection Strategy
Match your template to your industry culture:
Executive: Conservative industries value the sophisticated dark aesthetic
Startup: Creative and tech-forward roles benefit from the modern light design
Technical: Engineering and technical roles align with the modern technical aesthetic
Publishing and Sharing
Once you've generated your portfolio:
Use Netlify Drop for free, instant hosting (no credit card required)
Share the link in email signatures, LinkedIn, job applications, and resume
Update your portfolio before major job searches or after significant achievements

6. Troubleshooting & Tips
Photo Not Displaying
If your photo doesn't show in the downloaded file, ensure your image file is valid JPG/PNG. Try re-uploading with a smaller file size (under 2 MB).
Content Being Cut Off
Portify designs are optimized for professional summaries of 2-3 sentences. If your summary is longer, consider condensing to highlight only the most relevant information.
Template Colors Not Matching
Color appearance may vary slightly based on your monitor calibration. The actual colors are consistent across browsers. If you need custom colors, you can edit the downloaded HTML file directly.
Publishing with Netlify
To use Netlify Drop: (1) Go to drop.netlify.com, (2) Drag your downloaded portfolio.html file into the drop zone, (3) Your site instantly publishes with a shareable URL.
